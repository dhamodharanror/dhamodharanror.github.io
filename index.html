Engineering Lead, CultureMonkey

CultureMonkey is an employee engagement platform used by organisations worldwide to run surveys, track sentiment, and understand what drives their teams. I lead the backend engineering team that builds the systems the product runs on.

The work spans distributed systems design, multi-tenant architecture, third-party integrations, analytics pipelines, and auth infrastructure. Every tenant runs in its own isolated PostgreSQL schema. Background jobs carry tenant context automatically. Survey lifecycle, participant flows, and submission all operate within this model with zero cross-tenant exposure.

Outside the systems side, I spend a lot of time on the team itself — hiring, code review, technical direction, and making sure we're building the right things in the right order.

Builds

Most of what I've shipped lives inside CultureMonkey's product. Here's what I can describe.

  • Slack & Microsoft Teams integrations Built both from scratch — OAuth app install flows, survey delivery as interactive Slack messages and Teams adaptive cards, and response capture back into the platform. Participation improves when surveys reach people where they already work.
  • HRIS integration ecosystem A provider factory connecting to 10+ HR systems — BambooHR, Workday, Keka, Darwinbox, ZohoPeople, FreshService, and more. Handles first-sync, incremental sync, employee hierarchy mapping, and team structure. Adding a new connector doesn't touch existing ones.
  • Dual analytics pipeline (Elasticsearch + ClickHouse) Designed the move from pure Elasticsearch to a dual-pipeline architecture. Elasticsearch handles real-time search and segmentation. ClickHouse ingests on a 5-minute schedule for heavy aggregations — trend analysis, benchmark comparisons, cross-account analytics. Report query times dropped significantly.
  • SAML SSO + 2FA auth stack SAML 2.0 SP/IdP-initiated SSO with Google and custom enterprise IdPs, Microsoft OAuth, TOTP-based 2FA, CanCanCan role-based access control, and an internal Google SAML flow. Tenant-aware routing throughout.
