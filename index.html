Builds

Most of my work is inside CultureMonkey's product — things that either don't exist publicly or aren't obviously attributable. Here's a representative slice of what I've designed and shipped.

  • Slack & Microsoft Teams integrations
    Built both channel integrations from scratch — OAuth app install flows, survey delivery as interactive Slack messages and Teams adaptive cards, and response capture back into the platform. Participation improves when surveys reach employees where they already work.
  • HRIS integration ecosystem
    A provider factory that connects to 10+ HR systems — BambooHR, Workday, Keka, Darwinbox, ZohoPeople, FreshService, and more. Handles first-sync, incremental sync, employee hierarchy mapping, and team structure. Adding a new connector doesn't touch existing ones.
  • Dual analytics pipeline (Elasticsearch + ClickHouse)
    Designed the move from a pure Elasticsearch setup to a dual-pipeline architecture. Elasticsearch handles real-time search and segmentation. ClickHouse ingests on a 5-minute schedule for heavy aggregations — trend analysis, benchmark comparisons, cross-account analytics. Report query times dropped significantly.
  • Multi-tenant survey platform
    Every customer gets an isolated PostgreSQL schema via ros-apartment. Subdomain middleware handles tenant switching per request. Background jobs carry tenant context automatically. Survey lifecycle, participant flows, autosave, and submission all run within this model with zero cross-tenant exposure.
  • SAML SSO + 2FA auth stack
    SAML 2.0 SP/IdP-initiated SSO with Google and custom enterprise IdPs, Microsoft OAuth, TOTP-based 2FA via ROTP, CanCanCan role-based access control, and a separate CM Internal SAML flow. Tenant-aware routing throughout.
